FAQ'S

Christmas Food to Order is our online Christmas food service, where customers can pre-order their Christmas and New Year food,ready to collect from 22 December.
Due to unprecedented demand, we have to close our service early due to collection slot availability. We look forward to welcoming you into our stores where you can browse several of our Christmas Favourites, from centrepiece turkeys to delicious desserts.
Don’t worry, we will resend your order confirmation to you prior to your collection date. Remember, you can also view the items in your order or details of your collection by visiting ‘My Online Orders’ in the ‘Your Account’ section online.
Make sure you print off your email confirmation or have it on your phone — you’ll need it to collect your order. Don’t forget to bring some bags with you to take your festive feast home.
In the event of a lockdown, all customers who have placed an order will be notified via email of any changes on how order collections will take place in their chosen store.
If you are self-isolating, please nominate a friend or family member to collect on your behalf. Please ensure they bring your order confirmation number with them and bags to take home your festive feast.
You won’t be charged the full order amount; however the $40 deposit will remain non-refundable.
At Foodhall If you bought the item in one of our Foodhalls, please take your receipt and the packaging to a food information desk where one of our in-store colleagues will assist you. Unfortunately, if neither the receipt nor the packing have been retained then we cannot take this further.
Food to Order If you’re unhappy with your Food to Order, please visit your collection store or contact us.
If your Member card is registered to your online account, your order will qualify for Sparks – simply present your card at the time of ordering to be in for a chance of receiving little treats and thank yous. For more information on members card, please contact us.
